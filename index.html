Case 02
11 years of habits, one new department, and the resistance that came with it. Here's how it turned out.
A 25-person studio, 11 years on the market, no QA function. Developers were testing their own code. Bugs were reaching production.

I mapped every existing process, designed the department before hiring anyone, introduced the tooling stack gradually, and managed significant organizational resistance. Full implementation in 2.5 months.

Three years later the company still runs on the same processes. When a third product launched, QA absorbed it without disruption.

Identified and integrated the right AI tools across the full art pipeline, from concept to 3D modeling, texturing, rigging and animation, reducing production time by 30%. Connected Claude at organizational level for decision-making and execution speed, reducing product team overhead by 15%.

Currently building and training a full team of AI agents on the studio's own context to automate repetitive and time-consuming tasks across departments.
